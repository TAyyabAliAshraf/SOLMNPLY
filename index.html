About Solana Monopoly

Revolutionizing Prize Games on Solana

Solana Monopoly is the first token-based prize game built on the Solana blockchain, combining the thrill of winning with the power of decentralized finance.

Our innovative system automatically distributes prizes to holders every 5 hours, with no manual entry required. The more $SOLMNPLY tokens you hold, the greater your chances of winning.

With three distinct prize tiers (SOL jackpot, luxury flights, and premium gift cards), Solana Monopoly offers multiple ways to win while participating in the Solana ecosystem.

The project is community-driven with transparent, on-chain verification for all draws. Built using Solana's VRF (Verifiable Random Function), we ensure provably fair results for every participant.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join Solana Monopoly?

Simply purchase and hold $SOLMNPLY in your wallet. No additional registration is required. The system automatically detects eligible wallets based on token holdings.

How often are prizes awarded?

Prizes are distributed every 5 hours through a randomized draw. There are 4-5 draws each day, giving you multiple chances to win.

What types of rewards are available?

Three prize types: (1) A variable SOL jackpot funded by transaction fees, (2) A roundtrip airline ticket to any destination (up to $2,000 value), and (3) A $5,000 bundle in gift cards from retailers of your choice.

How are winners selected?

Each wallet is assigned a unique number based on their holdings. A random number is selected using Solana's VRF (Verifiable Random Function) which provides cryptographic proof that the draw was fair and unpredictable.

How do I claim my prize if I win?

SOL prizes are automatically sent to the winning wallet. For physical prizes (flights and gift cards), you'll receive instructions to a secure claim portal where you can provide your details and select your preferred options.
